# Global Style

CSS resets and base stylesheets definition.

```jsx
const App = () => (
  <>
    <GlobalStyle />
    <OtherComponents />
  </>
);
```

## Typoghraphy

Basic global Typoghraphy.

### Headings

All HTML headings, `<h1>` through `<h6>`, are avaliable:

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Headings">
    <div>
      <h1>Título 1 </h1>
      <h2>Título 2 </h2>
      <h3>Título 3 </h3>
      <h4>Título 4 </h4>
      <h5>Título 5 </h5>
      <h6>Título 6 </h6>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Canvas>

### Paragraph

Styling for common text elements:

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Paragraph">
    <div>
      <p>The quick brown fox over the lazy dog.</p>
      <p>
        <strong>The quick brown fox over the lazy dog. </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
        <em>The quick brown fox over the lazy dog. </em>
      </p>
      <p>
        <u>The quick brown fox over the lazy dog. </u>
      </p>
      <p>
        <s>The quick brown fox over the lazy dog. </s>
      </p>
    </div>
  </Story>
</Canvas>
